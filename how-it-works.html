How It Works

Application, removal, and reuse in three simple steps.

Application — Tabs (1–3 days)

  1. Prep: wash, dry, lightly buff nails.
  2. Apply tab, press on nail 30s.
  3. Avoid water for 1 hour.

Application — Glue (7–14 days)

  1. Buff and dehydrate nails (alcohol wipe).
  2. Small dot of glue; press 30s.
  3. Avoid hot water for 1 hour.

Removal & Reuse

  • Soak in warm water + oil; gently wiggle — no force.
  • Clean residual glue, store back in tray.
  • File old glue before next wear.
