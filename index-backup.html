    4 Supply Chain & Dropshipping

    🦋 Hybrid Supply Chain - Moth Wing Power Operations

    Structure: Maintain a curated catalog fulfilled via dropshipping partners (US/EU where possible) + limited, hand‑painted "Artist Series" crafted locally for higher AOV and content.

    📦 Dropshipping Structure

    • Catalogue: stocked SKUs via DS partners (US/EU when possible)
    • Artist Series: in‑house, made‑to‑order
    • QC: incoming samples + random batch tests
    • Finish testing: sizing, adhesion, durability

    🏭 Supplier Vetting

    • Lead time (processing + ship‑from locations)
    • Finish library (chrome, cat‑eye, matte, HEMA‑free)
    • Defect rate & remake policy (goal < 3% over 100 orders)
    • Packaging customization + insert printing
    • Moth wing pattern capability

    ⚡ Service Level Agreements

    • Stock SKUs: ship in 48h
    • MTO Artist Series: 5–7 biz days
    • Ticket response < 24h
    • DOA replacement in 7 days
    • Size swap: one free rematch set (first order)

    🚚 Logistics & Fulfillment

    • Label routing: branded sender with returns hub
    • US/EU warehouses prioritized for Q4 speed
    • Track & notify (email + SMS + FB/IG DM fallback)
    • Moth wing branded packaging
    • Tutorial QR codes in packaging

    ⚖️ Compliance & Safety - Moth Wing Power Standards

    Cosmetic Claims

    • Cosmetic claims only; avoid medical/therapeutic language
    • Adhesive labels: ingredients, warnings, manufacturer/shipper address
    • Tutorials: safe removal guidance; patch test advisory

    Regulatory Compliance

    • Prop 65/CPSIA review for CA/minors exposure if relevant
    • HEMA-free options clearly labeled
    • Age-appropriate warnings
