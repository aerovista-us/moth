How It Works

Application, removal, and reuse in three simple steps.

Application — Tabs (1–3 days)

  1. Prep: wash, dry, lightly buff nails.
  2. Apply tab, press on nail 30s.
  3. Avoid water for 1 hour.

Application — Glue (7–14 days)

  1. Buff and dehydrate nails (alcohol wipe).
  2. Small dot of glue; press 30s.
  3. Avoid hot water for 1 hour.

Removal & Reuse

  • Soak in warm water + oil; gently wiggle — no force.
  • Clean residual glue, store back in tray.
  • File old glue before next wear.

🎨 Product Overview

🦋 Brand System - "Brush & Edge" by Moth Empire

Tagline: Nail art as your canvas - Where Beauty Takes Flight

Voice: Confident, artistic, instructive. Tutorial-friendly copy with playful sophistication and moth wing power.

Visuals: Soft neutrals with iridescent accents. Macro nail shots, painterly strokes, subtle chrome flares inspired by moth wing patterns.

🎨 Collection Architecture

  • Signature Art Sets (core catalogue)
  • Accent/Remix Packs (2–4 add‑on nails)
  • Artist Series (numbered, hand‑painted)
  • Seasonal Drops (monthly limited themes)
  • Pet‑Friendly Collection (Paws & Polish, Buddy's Brush)

🏷️ SKU Model

Format: NAIL-[STYLE]-[LENGTH]-[FINISH]-[DESIGN]-[SIZEKIT]

Example: NAIL-ALMOND-M-GLOSS-MILKY-S12

Artist Series: ART-NUMBERED-HANDPAINT-XX

🎨 Design Concepts

  • Milky Canvas + Minimal Accent - Base: sheer milk/cream; tip accent
  • Cat‑Eye / Iridescent Chrome - Magnetic cat‑eye gel; accent swirl
  • Bold French (Texture Twist) - Nude base; colored tips with micro motif
  • Nature & 3D Accents - Pearls, dew‑drops, tiny blossoms
  • Deep Tones + Mirror - Wine/plum/emerald; mirror foil accents

🦋 Moth Wing Power Features

  • Art-first: hand‑painted details, not commodity prints
  • Care-first: sizing kits, tutorial QR, reuse options
  • Limited: numbered small batches for hype cycles
  • Premium finishes: chrome, cat‑eye, glass, 3D accents
